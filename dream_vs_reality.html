Dream vs reality.

By Jakub Brehuv, xx.8.2018

Did you ever have that feeling that you got a great question to ask somebody, but this question was so gerat that you want to give answer more that hear one. That is exact feeling I have right now. For last couple of months I was trying to improve my dialogues with people. That mean I wanted to be more present in that moments, don't let anything distract me. It worked really good but I find out that If I want to go deeper and get to know person on another level I better have some good questions ready in my pocket. Don't take me wrong, talking about gym, coding and some other shuff is great but only until certain point. The one question that stand above all other questions I was thinking about goes like this: "What is the most astonishing thing in life. What amaze you every time you see it, experience it, hear it or maybe feel it. Whatever it is. "
